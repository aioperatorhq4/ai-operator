# AI Operator Content Strategy

## Primary Topic

Artificial Intelligence

## Categories

1. AI Tools
2. AI Business
3. AI Automation
4. AI Agents
5. AI News

## Target Audience

- Entrepreneurs
- Operators
- Business owners
- Consultants
- Freelancers

## Content Rules

Every article must answer one of these questions:

- How can AI save time?
- How can AI make money?
- How can AI reduce costs?
- How can AI improve operations?
- How can AI increase productivity?

## Content To Avoid

- Politics
- Celebrity news
- General world news
- Entertainment
- Sports
- Gossip

## Success Metric

Create content that helps readers implement AI in real businesses.
